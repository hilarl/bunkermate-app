The platform for yachts

BunkerMate is a free, no-obligation, web-based solution platform that connects yachts with their fuel suppliers and saves you money. By buying directly from the supplier, relationships are built. Build and manage your network with BunkerMate. See what others say about your potential fuel supplier before you pick. Bunkers are approximately 10/15% of the operational cost of a yacht. This cost item deserves special attention, from both a financial and trust perspective. By using BunkerMate you have control over these aspects. Manage all your fuel operation in one central place. A clear and complete overview with all suppliers shown in an interactive map. Automated system which gives you a price estimation directly and lets you set the delivery date.

Where do you find a trustworthy organisation that is transparent about the pricing and commissions? It's a complicated question and online availability is limited, pricing is on request and there are also no reliable reviews. If you start searching online, you will only reach the tussenpersonen and they earn sometimes over 50% on the original price. I knew how much yachts are struggling to save on operational costs. It was (about) time for BunkerMate, the online platform where you can order fuel in a few clicks. On your phone, tablet or computer. You see the prices immediately, nothing on request, personal contact with your favorite supplier and you can pay online. After the fuel is being delivered you leave a review, we all know that from previous websites.

Vision

To create a centralized, transparent platform where availability is being answered, regardless which port. It’s our mission to create an environment where you connect with the right person, in one click.
